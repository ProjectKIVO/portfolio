Hello! I am an undergraduate student studying computer science at Purdue University. I aspire to be a research and educator with a focus on interdisciplinary applications of quantum algorithms and their practical usability. My research generally focuses on the intersection between quantum computing, computer graphics, and HCI, with the goal of simplifying novelty for accessibility and usability.

I was fortunate enough to work with wonderful advisors and colleagues and contributed to multiple research projects, including optimization algorithms for procedural generation and Rdyberg atom inspired data preprocessing. Furthermore, I am also interested in extended reality (VR/XR/AR) related research alognside procedural geometry.
